# Sorted — for tradespeople

> **Stop chasing customers. Get paid before you turn up.**

## What it does

Sorted gives you a personal booking page customers can use 24/7. They
pick a slot, pay a deposit, you get a booking. No phone tag, no
chasing, no "I forgot you were coming."

When you're heading to a job, tap "On the way" — the customer gets a
text with your ETA and a live tracking link. When you mark the job
complete, the app generates an invoice with their deposit deducted and
sends a payment link.

## What it solves

- **No-shows.** Customers who put down a deposit show up. Or they
  forfeit it.
- **Time-wasters.** Anyone who isn't serious doesn't get past the
  deposit. You stop chasing.
- **Late payment.** Invoice is sent automatically the moment the job's
  done. Pay-by-card link goes straight to the customer's phone.
- **Looking unprofessional.** Your booking page looks better than 90% of
  trade sites you've seen — without you having to build anything.

## How it works for you

1. Sign up. Connect your Stripe account (takes 5 minutes).
2. Pick from pre-built services (boiler service, leak repair, etc.) or
   add your own.
3. Set your working hours.
4. Share your booking link via WhatsApp, text, email, or stick a QR
   code on your van.
5. Customers book. Deposits arrive in your bank account.
6. You run the day from your phone.

## How it works for your customers

1. They tap your link.
2. They pick a service and a time.
3. They enter their details, pay a deposit, done.
4. They get a confirmation. The trade can text them when on the way
   with a live tracker. The trade marks complete and an invoice arrives
   for the balance.

## What it costs

- **Free for the first 60 days.** No card required.
- **After that: £19/month** for unlimited bookings.
- **Stripe takes their normal fees** on each deposit (1.5% + 20p UK
  cards). Not us. Stripe pays you directly.

If you do more than 13 jobs a month with a deposit, you're already
saving money. Most plumbers do 50+.
